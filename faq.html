What is Futurithm?
Futurithm is a futures trading education platform built to help beginners and prop firm traders learn through structure, discipline, and a clear progression path.
Is Futurithm beginner friendly?
Yes. The platform is designed to guide complete beginners from basic futures concepts into more advanced execution and funded-trader preparation.
What is the Futurithm Method?
The Futurithm Method is the flagship course focused on market rhythm, structure, liquidity, execution, and risk discipline.
What is the Futurithm Trading Floor?
It is the private community where members can access discussions, live sessions, strategy breakdowns, and additional support.
What is a prop firm challenge?
A prop firm challenge is an evaluation process where traders must follow rules and hit performance goals to qualify for a funded account.
Is Futurithm financial advice?
No. Futurithm is an educational platform only and does not provide financial or investment advice.
